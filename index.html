Quantum Dynamics in the Condensed Phase

Path Integrals for open quantum systems

Chemical processes are governed by the fundamental rules of quantum mechanics. Simulating these systems accurately is a very challenging task owing to the exponential scaling of quantum dynamics with the number of particles. My work primarily focuses on developing accurate and approximate techniques to overcome this severe limitation, and to enable the study of quantum transport in large, condensed phase systems.
